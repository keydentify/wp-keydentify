== Description ==
Secure your datas and your users accounts with [Keydentify](http://www.keydentify.com).

= What is Keydentify? =

[Keydentify](http://www.keydentify.com) is a tokenless multi-factor user authentication. After a traditional password-based authentication, you just have to take one more second to launch your Keydentify mobile app and confirm your identity.

You can enable / disable Keydentify service for each of your user accounts

For simplicity and efficiency, [Keydentify](http://www.keydentify.com) allows you to:
- Use a single app to secure access to multiple sites,
- Have multiple and synchronized paired devices,
- Share one or more identification keys with other users.

== Installation ==
1. Unzip and upload `/wp-keydentify/` to `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Activate your website on https://www.keydentify.me and get your credentials
4. Put your credentials in Keydentify admin
5. Activate Keydentify for your account, disconnect and reconnect
